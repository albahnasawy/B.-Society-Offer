Basic Food Safety Course Offer - B. Society

This document outlines the offer for a Basic Food Safety Course provided by "B. Society," designed to enhance food safety knowledge and practices across multiple branches.

Course Overview

  • Course Title: Basic Food Safety Course
  • Duration: 12 hours, spread across 3 sessions over three months.
  • Locations: Training will be conducted across three branches:
    • Mansoura 1
    • Mansoura 2
    • Tanta
  • Target Audience: Senior Management and Junior's Team at each branch.
  • Training Format: On-site training, 1 day per branch per session.
